PROJECTS

SmartBasket

Created at Walmart's Brick & Code 2.0 Hackathon, this iOS application allows shoppers to participate in "shop sharing," an opportunity to combine multiple users' orders and save money.

Adopt.Me

It's Tinder, but for dogs! This iOS application utilizes the popular Tinder interface to connect those looking to adopt a pet with dogs & cats at their local shelters. Swiping right on an animal allows you to show interest, providing you with additional pet info and an opportunity to contact the shelter.

Knock Knock

Dorm life can be awkward, especially when it comes to privacy. Knock Knock lets you ask your roommate(s) for some private time with a quick tap on your phone, avoiding awkward conversations and giving you the space you need. Privacy options include sleep, study, and me time.
